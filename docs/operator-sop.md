# CountLock V1 Operator SOP

## Purpose

CountLock prevents missing or extra parts from being placed into kits.

## Setup

1. Open CountLock on the phone or tablet.
2. Confirm the camera is mounted and pointing at the counting mat.
3. Confirm the mat is clear.
4. Select the project.
5. Select the kit.

## Counting process

For each part group:

1. Place the current group on the mat.
2. Spread the pieces apart.
3. Tap **Picture**.
4. Read the detected count.
5. If the count is wrong, fix the layout and tap **Picture** again.
6. Move that group into the kit.
7. Continue to the next group.

## Buttons

### Back

Moves to the previous part group.

### Picture

Captures the mat and counts visible objects.

### Finished

Locks the kit and sends review results.

## Rules

1. Do not overlap parts.
2. Do not mix different groups.
3. Do not tap Finished until every group has been counted.
4. If the count keeps failing, notify the supervisor.
